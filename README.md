# TokaGames

This repository contains the source files for educational games developed by the TokaLab team and external collaborators.

# Developed Games

## TokaCards

TokaCards is a fast-paced word-guessing card game inspired by classic party games.
The goal is simple: help your team guess the target word â€” but each mode changes the rules and the challenge.

TokaCards features three main modes:

**Forbidden**  
Inspired by taboo-style games. Help your team guess the main word without using the five forbidden words listed on the card.  

**Mime It**  
No speaking. No sounds. Only gestures. Act it out and let your team figure it out.  

**Draw It**  
Sketch the word on paper without writing letters, numbers, or symbols. Your drawing skills will be put to the test.

Each mode pushes your creativity in a different way, making every round dynamic and unpredictable.

### How to use it

TokaCards is free, open access, and open source.

You can:

Play directly through our [browser app](https://tokalab.github.io/games/tokacards/) on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

Use the available [Python script](https://github.com/TokaLab/TokaGames/tree/main/TokaCards) to generate printable card images and play offline with physical copies.
You can also create your own cards and expand the game by adding new words using our tools.


Play online. Print it. Customise it.
TokaCards is designed to be flexible, accessible, and easy to share.
